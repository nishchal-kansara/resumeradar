About Project

About ResumeRadar

It is an AI-powered platform designed to simplify your job application journey - from resume analysis to interview preparation. Whether you're starting from scratch or improving your existing resume, ResumeRadar gives you smart tools to get noticed by recruiters and beat ATS (Applicant Tracking Systems).
And here's the best part? We don't store any of your personal data. Your uploaded files are auto-deleted from our server within 10-15 minutes. Privacy first, always.

What We Do?
  1. At ResumeRadar, we help you understand and improve your resume like never before. You begin by uploading your resume on the homepage. Our system instantly analyzes it and compares it with a job description you provide. This gives you a detailed breakdown of keyword matching, skills gap, experience alignment, and even your ATS compatibility score - so you know where you stand before applying.
  2. But we don't stop there. You can rebuild your resume using insights from the analysis. Either refine your existing one to make it ATS-friendly or create a brand new one from scratch by simply answering a few basic questions. This makes it easy for anyone - student, fresher, or experienced professional - to create a polished, professional resume in minutes.
  3. Need a cover letter? We've got you. Just input your resume and the job description, and we'll instantly generate a tailored cover letter that matches the role. And when it's time to face the interview, you can use our mock interview feature to prepare. Based on your resume and the job description, we'll generate relevant questions so you can practice confidently.
Why Choose ResumeRadar?

We built ResumeRadar to take the guesswork out of job applications. Our tools are fast, smart, and built for real people - not just recruiters. We support job seekers at all stages - students, freshers, experienced candidates, and those switching careers - by giving them personalized support through every step of the application process.

Our Mission

At ResumeRadar, our mission is to simplify the resume creation process, making it accessible and efficient for everyone. Whether you're entering the job market for the first time or looking to make a career switch, we're here to help you present your best self to potential employers.

Who We Serve?
  1. Interns: Building a resume with limited experience? No worries! We'll help you create a resume that focuses on your skills, education, and potential.
  2. Freshers: Just graduated and looking for your first job? Our platform helps you highlight your academic achievements, projects, and any relevant experience.
  3. Intermediate Professionals: Ready to move up the ladder? Showcase your growing career and accomplishments with a polished, professional resume.
  4. Experienced Professionals: With years of experience, you want a resume that makes an impact. Our tool ensures that your resume is ATS-optimized and highlights your career progression.
